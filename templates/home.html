Guardian

Guardian uses Twilio, a cloud communications platform, to keep our loved ones safe. When a user is moving from place to place, Guardian regularly checks in on them via SMS messaging, to ensure that the user is safe and notifies emergency contacts in any case otherwise. This way, friends and family can always be sure they know of their loved ones' wellbeing.
